About Me

I am an Associate Professor at Computer School, Wuhan University. I received my Ph.D. degree in Computer Science at The Chinese University of Hong Kong in 2013. Before that, I received my bachelor's and master's degrees in Computer Science and Technology from Harbin Institute of Technology in 2007 and 2009 respectively.

My main research areas are Database Techniques, Data Mining, and Data Analysis. Currently I am doing research on Query and Mining in Graph Database, Social Network Analysis, BigData Management and Analysis, and Software Data Mining.
